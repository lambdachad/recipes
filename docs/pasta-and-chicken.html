Pasta and Chicken

Ingredients

  • Penne Pasta [link]500 gEGP 17
  • Chicken Breast [link]500 gEGP 131
  • Potato [link]500 gEGP 6
  • Oil [link]200 mlEGP 22
  • Cooking Cream [link]500 mlEGP 144
  • Chicken Spices
  • Salt
  • Garlic Powder
  • TotalEGP 321

Steps

1. Cook 500 g pasta until al dente, then drain. 2. Cut 500 g chicken breast into dices. Cook in a pan with chicken spices, salt, and garlic powder until golden and cooked through. 3. Peel and cube 500 g potatoes. Fry in 200 ml oil until golden and crispy. 4. Add 500 ml cooking cream to the chicken, stir, and let it simmer for a few minutes. 5. Add the potatoes and pasta, toss everything together, and serve.
