Roasted Chicken

Ingredients

  • Whole Chicken [link]1EGP 170
  • Oil [link]50 mlEGP 6
  • Lemon [link]1EGP 2
  • Salt2 tbsp
  • Paprika1 tbsp
  • Garlic Powder1 tbsp
  • Onion Powder1 tbsp
  • Dried Oregano1 tsp
  • TotalEGP 178

Steps

1. Drain 1 whole chicken for 10 minutes, then dry well with paper towels. 2. Rub 50 ml oil all over the chicken. 3. Mix 2 tbsp salt, 1 tbsp paprika, 1 tbsp garlic powder, 1 tbsp onion powder, and 1 tsp dried oregano. Rub all over the chicken, inside and out. 4. Cut 1 lemon in half and place inside the chicken. 5. Optionally, leave in the fridge for up to 12 hours. 6. Preheat oven to 200°C. Roast for 10 minutes, then lower to 180°C and roast until the breast reaches 75°C. 7. Let it rest, then cut and pour the pan juices over the meat.
